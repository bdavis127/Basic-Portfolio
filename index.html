Introduction

Hello, and welcome to my professional portfolio. My education in web development has come primarily from self study as well as my recent graduation from Southern Methodist University's coding bootcamp in Dallas, Texas.

My current fascination is web development, most specifically UX Design. I enjoy working with the many aspects of creation involved, such as creating wireframes and prototypes leading up to a finished product based on specific user desires collected through research. I wish to apply my experience and knowledge I have gathered in order to further the abilities of users who desire a positive online experience.

In today's digital environment, the online world is a busy place. Every business and service out there is constantly demanding the attention of potential customers. Proper use of interface design isn't just helpful anymore. It is absolutely necessary. If you are in need of UX design services, please feel free to reach out to me using my Contact page.
