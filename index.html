About Me

Hello, and welcome to my bio page. It is wonderful to be able to reach out via the World Wide Web to all who are interested in learning of my experiences.

On this journey, I have lived overseas in Europe and traveled across the United States. I have met people along the way that truly influenced how I see the world. Not only do I enjoy working among diverse groups, I feel it is very necessary to be willing and able to work and live among people of diverse cultures and backgrounds.

Before venturing overseas, I earned a Bachelors degree in Psychology and a Masters Degree in Instructional Technology. I have worked in the fields of Software Training, Instructional Design, and Research. I am currently in the process of broadening my horizons by furthering my knowledge of web development. My goal is to become a web developer and apply my experience where it is useful in helping organizations to better reach those in need of their services.

When I'm not striving to learn the latest trends in online development, I like to practice disciplines that help to maintain my physical and mental awareness, such as yoga and meditation. I have also volunteered my services for the Amrit Yoga Institute in Florida in order to give back where I have received great knowledge of spiritual well being.
